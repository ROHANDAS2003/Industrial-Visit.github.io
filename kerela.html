Seven Day Goa Industrial Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Kochi Arrive at Kochi International Airport. Meet our representative and transfer to your hotel. Explore the Fort Kochi area, known for its colonial architecture, Chinese fishing nets, and spice markets. Visit the iconic Mattancherry Palace and the Jewish Synagogue. Enjoy an evening Kathakali dance performance. Overnight stay in Kochi.

Day 2: Kochi Sightseeing After breakfast, visit the Hill Palace Museum, a former royal residence with a vast collection of artifacts and antiques. Explore the Dutch Palace, also known as the Mattancherry Palace, showcasing Kerala-style architecture and murals. Visit the St. Francis Church, the oldest European church in India. Take a relaxing stroll along Marine Drive, a popular waterfront promenade. Overnight stay in Kochi.

Day 3: Kochi to Munnar After breakfast, check out from your hotel in Kochi and drive to Munnar. En route, visit the Cheeyappara and Valara waterfalls. Arrive in Munnar and check-in to your hotel. Explore the tea plantations and enjoy the scenic beauty of Munnar. Visit the Tea Museum to learn about tea processing and sample different varieties. Overnight stay in Munnar.

Day 4: Munnar Sightseeing After breakfast, visit the Eravikulam National Park, home to the endangered Nilgiri Tahr. Explore the beautiful Echo Point and take a boat ride in Kundala Lake. Visit the Mattupetty Dam and the surrounding tea gardens. Enjoy a traditional Ayurvedic massage for relaxation. Overnight stay in Munnar.

Day 5: Munnar to Alappuzha (Houseboat) After breakfast, drive to Alappuzha, also known as Alleppey. Check-in to a traditional Kerala houseboat and embark on a backwater cruise through the picturesque network of canals. Enjoy the scenic views, local village life, and delicious Kerala cuisine served on board. Overnight stay on the houseboat.

Day 6: Alappuzha to Trivandrum (Industry Visit) Disembark from the houseboat after breakfast. Drive to Trivandrum (Thiruvananthapuram), the capital city of Kerala. Visit a pre-arranged industry for an insightful industrial tour in Trivandrum. Learn about the industrial processes, technologies, and the contribution of the industry to the local economy. Overnight stay in Trivandrum.

Day 7: Trivandrum Sightseeing and Departure After breakfast, explore Trivandrum city. Visit the iconic Padmanabhaswamy Temple, known for its intricate architecture and religious significance. Explore the Napier Museum and Art Gallery, showcasing a rich collection of ancient artifacts and artworks. Enjoy a visit to the beautiful Kovalam Beach and relax by the Arabian Sea. In the evening, drop-off at Trivandrum Railway Station for your onward journey.

Note: The itinerary can be customized based on your preferences and the time of your visit. The specific industry for the visit in Trivandrum will depend on availability and your field of interest.
